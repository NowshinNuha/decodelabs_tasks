Project 2: SEO Fundamentals & Keyword Research - DecodeLabs


🎯 Executive Niche Strategy: David vs. Goliath
Instead of going up against massive aggregator platforms like Internshala by targeting hyper-competitive broad head terms (e.g., "Internship"), DecodeLabs wins by executing a hyper-targeted long-tail technical keyword approach. By targeting highly specific intent gaps, we secure low-competition, high-conversion organic search traffic.


🧭 The 4 Archetypes of Intent Strategy
We have mapped our target domain against search intents to capture users at varying stages of the curiosity lifecycle:
1. Informational: Capturing searchers looking to build basic tech knowledge.
2. Navigational: Directly guiding users who are explicitly looking for DecodeLabs properties.
3. Commercial: Engaging candidates evaluating the best tech learning stacks for 2026.
4. Transactional: Driving conversions for ready-to-act candidates seeking immediate industry placements.
